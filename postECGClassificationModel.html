Master's Degree Disstertation Project

The aim of this research is to develop a highly accurate deep learning model based on bagging techniques for 12-lead ECG classification, specifically targeting four types of rhythms, to improve diagnostic efficiency and accessibility in cardiac health monitoring. The underlying hypothesis is that ECG classification can be enhanced by implementing a bagging-based ensemble approach applied to various deep learning models.
