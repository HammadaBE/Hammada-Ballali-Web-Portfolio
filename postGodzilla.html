Godzilla Game in processing

This was a project I did as part of my firts year coursework. I was the sole creator/developer of the project when I was learning processing.

The game is based on controlling an image that represents Godzilla as he tries to catch all the cars before they disappear beyond the screen.

At the same time, some logical processes are implemented, such as the colors of the traffic light or the point counter.
