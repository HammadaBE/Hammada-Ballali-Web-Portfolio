RENT A CAR ANDROID APP

Rent A Car App is an Android app built in Kotlin and XML using the Android Studio IDE. The APP allows the user to log in to an account, choose a car to book, choose the booking dates, calculate the total amount and store the bookings in a list view.



It was done in 2023.
